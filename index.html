About CardioVision

All the features needed by a cardiology specialist bundled in one compact application.


CardioVision is a specialized healthcare-management platform, specifically designed to automate the process of echocardiographic image analysis. Through CardioVision, medical professionals can upload echocardiographic images & videos and retrieve critical insights and parameters important in determining the risks of cardiovascular diseases, with the help of our two-stage image classification-segmentation pipeline.

  • Automatically process echocardiographic videos

    Our built-in image classification and segmentation pipeline ensures that correct cardiographic view is uploaded, and then it efficiently segments the corresponding chambers of the heart to compute critical cardiovascular metrics like EF-rate and heartbeat. No more manual interference by doctors!

  • Keep and manage patients and echocardiographic videos in one place

    Automatically store echocardiographic videos on cloud and access them again later to display and analyze retrieved insights based on these videos. Also, list the echocardiographic videos associated with a specific patient easily.

  • Make informed decisions via our rich video analysis dashboard

    Watch the echocardiographic video frame by frame, and manually adjust the automatically-segmented regions of the heart for your own need to immediately observe the effects on EF-rate and heartbeat.

Features

A brief breakdown of the features of CardioVision

Automatic Calculation of EF-rate and Heartbeat

CardioVision uses advanced AI models to calculate EF-rate and Heartbeat from echocardiography videos.

Searching Patient

Thanks to our search feature, doctors can find the patient easily and save time.

Analyzing Echocardiography Video

CardioVision allows frame-by-frame analysis so doctors can see heart chamber sizes in detail.

Modifying Detected Edges

If doctors think the edges of the heart chambers are not detected perfectly, they can use our interactive system to correct the edges manually.
